### [First Draft's 7 types of mis- and disinformation](https://firstdraftnews.com/fake-news-complicated/)

In a scale that measures the intent to decive:

![](https://firstdraftnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/FDN_7Types_Misinfo-01-1024x576.jpg)

1. Satire or Parody - 諷刺、惡搞、反串
1. False Connection - 標題或圖片與內容不符
1. Misleading Content - 誤導、陷害之內容
1. False Context - 斷章取義、移花接木（上下文）
1. Imposter Content - 仿冒來源
1. Manipulated Content - 偽造、惡意改作、移花接木（內容本身）
1. Fabricated Content - 完全錯誤、意欲誤導或傷害之內容

## Efforts from Other Countries


### Turn Back Hoax (FAFHH) @ Indonesia

An NGO with 40,000 collaborators doing fact check!

It works in the following way:

1. Posts articles to be fact-checked to Facebook group https://www.facebook.com/groups/fafhh
2. Discussions initiated in facebook group as simple as replying to a post
3. Volunteers summarizes the discussion into an article to post in [Turn Back Hoax website](https://data.turnbackhoax.id/#/result/web/@terbaru).



### Sure and Share Center (Thai)


https://www.facebook.com/SureAndShare
http://www.tnamcot.com/sureandshare/

Operated by MCOT (Thai state-owned public broadcaster), they owns a Facebook fan page, an official website and a LINE@ account. After fact checking, they also publish infographics and mythbusting videos in both their website and their LINE@ timeline.

They use 1-on-1 chats in LINE@ to collect rumors, and uses a custom keyboard app populated with prerecorded responses to respond to the message senders.

We are reaching out to them to see if they are interested in building a bot. If so, we can provide technical support to them.
